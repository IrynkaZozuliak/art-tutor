ABOUT ME

I'm Penny, a Practicing Artist & Graphic Designer.

Since an early age I have been truly interested in the fine arts; sketching, design, painting and drawing. I am and have always been interested in life drawing and have spent a lot of time studying the human anatomy to apply this knowledge in my work.

I am sure that everyone can draw and paint, and do it quite well. Meeting different people and sharing my skills with them brings me more inspiration and satisfaction.

Currently, I am a teacher at LQ School where I conduct art and design workshops, organize competitions and work with students. I have also been tutoring privately.

My teaching experiences include:

●Conducting workshops for kids (aged 3-6);

●Art & design workshops for teens (age group of children 10-16);

●Teaching art at in small groups children and grown-ups;

●Organizing art events (workshops, meet-the-artist sessions) for students;

●Providing one-to-one tuition to children, teens and adults of various ages and skills, including students who are going to take a GCSE art exam, as well as art enthusiasts.

●Assistance in the preparation of exhibition works, art events.
