HOW TO START

4 Simple Steps to Start Drawing Like a Pro.

Even if you've never held a pencil or brush, in 3-6 months you'll be able to create beautiful paintings.

1. Talk to me.

Contact me by filling out the form below or just call me. Tell me about yourself, skills and the desired result.

2. Choose a Lesson.

I offer one-to-one lessons, small groups and workshops. You choose the most convenient way to study.

3. Reserve a Spot.

Choose the most suitable date and time for a trial lesson. If you like it, we'll decide on the usual schedule.

4. Let's Meet!

Come and meet me in my studio. You don't need to take anything with you, except for good mood.
