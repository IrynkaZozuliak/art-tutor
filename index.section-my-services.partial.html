MY SERVICES

Tutoring Options.

Fitting your timetable

I offer structured one-to-one courses with the flexibility to choose time slots. You can choose your starting date, day and time of the week when to attend the lessons.

Bespoke tuition

The structure, length of the lesson, the techniques and approaches I teach are tailored to your needs and skills. My goal is to develop your creativity in your unique way.

Any knowledge level

Whether you are a beginner, intermediate, or advanced, I devise a perfect tuition plan according to your needs, and in 3-6 months you'll be able to draw fairly well.

Adults & kids

My one-to-one classes are open to everyone, from children (4-yo minimum) to university students and grown-ups who want to boost their creativity skills.

Different techniques

I can teach you how to handle a variety of media, from drawing materials to oil, acrylic, and watercolors. Tell me about your favorite style and I'll teach you it.

Lots of fun

Do you want to attend classes with friends? Or do you need a fun activity for family weekends? Art creates great memories that last a life time.

Special events

From time to time I hold classes in my studio. You can sign up in advance and learn a lot about the different techniques of painting.
